# Welcome to Spirit Connect Group

Spirit Connect Group is my open and collaborative space for power electronics, AI-assisted engineering, creative systems, and long-term technical knowledge sharing.

The group connects two main directions: a public-facing landing page for the broader Spirit Connect ecosystem, and Spirit Connect AIPE Labs for open-source AI + power electronics research tools.
