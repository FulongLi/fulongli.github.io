## Overview

Solid state transformers combine power electronics, high-frequency isolation, and advanced control to create flexible interfaces between medium-voltage and low-voltage energy systems.

<img class="research-hero-image" src="/images/research/generated/solid-state-transformers.png" alt="Solid state transformer technical illustration" />

This topic is related to modular converter design, bidirectional power flow, smart-grid integration, and future distribution architectures with DC and AC outputs.
