# Research Areas

## **Power Electronics Converters**
- AI-assisted optimal design of DC/DC converters (buck, boost, DAB, LLC)  
- EMI/thermal-aware optimisation of magnetic components (planar inductors, PCB windings)  
- Characterisation of IGBT and SiC/GaN MOSFETs  
- High-bandwidth current sensing (PCB Rogowski coils, PCB embedded CTs)

## **Converter Control & Stability**
- Closed-loop control: double-loop, passivity-based, feedforward  
- Impedance modelling and stability analysis  
- Stability assessment in DC microgrids

## **DC Microgrids & Systems**
- Hierarchical control strategies & distributed energy management  
- EMS design (PV, battery systems)  
- Adaptive PV control & MPPT  
- Power scheduling and optimisation
