On **17 August 2019**, I successfully defended my PhD thesis. I am very happy and grateful—this is one of the great milestones of my life.

## Thank you

I would like to thank everyone who was part of my PhD journey. My thanks go to colleagues and friends from **Canada**, **China**, the **UK**, and **Aston University**—and above all, to **my supervisor**, who supported and encouraged me throughout the entire process. I am truly grateful for everybody who appeared in my life during this time.

## My PhD topic

My thesis focused on **DC control strategies for DC microgrids**. I believe this topic is very useful for future power distribution networks, and I hope it will become a growing trend— attracting more attention and support for research in this area. I hope my work will be useful to both industry and academia.

📄 [View my PhD thesis](https://publications.aston.ac.uk/id/eprint/46135/) — *Control Strategies for Low Voltage DC Microgrids*, Aston University, UK.
