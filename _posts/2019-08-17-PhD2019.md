On **17 August 2019**, I successfully defended my PhD thesis. It was a memorable milestone and a great moment to look back on the work, the challenges, and the people who supported me along the way.

## What this milestone means to me

Completing a PhD is not just finishing a document—it is learning how to define meaningful questions, build reliable methods, and communicate results clearly. The defense was the final step of a long process of research, iteration, and growth.

## Gratitude

I’m sincerely grateful to:

- **My supervisor(s)** for guidance, patience, and high standards
- **My thesis committee/examiners** for their time and constructive feedback
- **My colleagues and collaborators** for discussions and support
- **My family and friends** for encouragement throughout the journey

## Looking ahead

I’m excited to keep building on this work and to continue researching and engineering solutions in my field.
